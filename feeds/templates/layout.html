Tweets Di Feed

This simple Application will enable you to get RSS Feeds of tweets of people you follow.

Because of rate limiting policies of twitter it takes time to compile the list of people you follow so we will DM you the link to OPML file as and when the process finishes.

With respect to APP permissions, to DM you the details we will need highest of them. Otherwise we could had done with minimum permissions to get list of people you follow and their latest tweets.
